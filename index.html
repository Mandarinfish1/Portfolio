As a current attendee of the University of Richmond Coding Bootcamp, I am acquiring a comprehensive range of technical skills in front-end and back-end technologies, including the application of algorithms (such as searches and sorts), considerations of efficiency and time complexity, and the use of Big O notation. Moreover, I am familiarizing myself with an array of data structures, such as arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues, trees, and graphs. Through this bootcamp, I am also mastering browser-based technologies, such as HTML/CSS, JavaScript/jQuery, and responsive design frameworks like Bootstrap and Handlebars. Additionally, I am becoming proficient in React.js, MySQL, MongoDB, and server-side development, as well as user authentication, building RESTful APIs, and working with Node.js. I am also learning about the development of progressive web applications (PWAs), such as the use of MERN Stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) and API interaction with JSON and AJAX. Furthermore, I am currently creating a comprehensive portfolio that showcases my proficiency in these areas, featuring a range of projects I have developed during the bootcamp. This portfolio includes my resume and links to reach me, allowing potential employers to explore my work in detail and consider me as a candidate for future opportunities.
